Inclusivity

Accessibility Statement

We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all corporate commuters, including those with disabilities.

Our Standards

CommuteSync targets conformity with WCAG 2.1 Level AA web guidelines. Our system features high color-contrast ratios, keyboard focus indicators, screen-reader semantic hierarchies, and scalable layouts.

Assistive Accommodations

Commuters with special vehicle requirement profiles (such as wheelchair access or accessible onboarding routes) can declare these preferences when searching or publishing rides, ensuring compatible matches across departments.
