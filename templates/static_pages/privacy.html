Data Protection

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. Here is how we collect, store, and process your travel and profile information.

1. Information Collection

We collect route parameters (pickup and destination coordinates), vehicle info, profile details (first name, last name, phone, email, and employee ID), and transaction settlement histories. Live GPS locations are tracked solely during active transits.

2. How We Use Data

We utilize details to calculate matches, calculate route distances, prevent fraud, generate security OTP codes, and authorize payments. Location sharing is exclusively enabled between passenger and driver during matched trips.

3. Data Sharing

Your personal credentials (name, phone, email) are private and are shared only with the matched commuter during ride allocations. We do not sell or monetize commuter information to third-party advertisers.
