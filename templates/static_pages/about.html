Our Story

About CommuteSync

CommuteSync is a premium intra-corporate carpooling network designed to turn separate, gridlocked employee commutes into unified, shared journeys.

Our Mission

We strive to optimize daily transit to office parks, mitigate traffic congestion, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and connect colleagues across departments to build closer work relationships. By aligning similar route vectors, we ensure that every empty seat is utilized efficiently.

How It Works

Employees with active vehicles can publish their planned route and departure schedules. Coworkers traveling along similar routes can search, request to join, coordinate in real-time using our chatbot, and secure matches within seconds. Commute fare settlements are seamlessly handled using digital wallets or UPI gateways.
